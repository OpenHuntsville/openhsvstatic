OpenHSV was conceived as a free community resource for Huntsville's startup and small business population. Our goal was to create an open directory of individuals and firms who are willing to offer their services to help fellow Huntsville-based businesses grow. Any individual professional or group of professionals is welcome to create a listing on OpenHSV. Together, we can boost our economy and help our local business community achieve new heights.

Currently, OpenHSV is home to professionals representing a limited number of industries and backgrounds. If you work in an industry that is unrepresented on OpenHSV, please suggest it by giving us feedback.

OpenHSV was designed and built by Andrew Hall, Joe MacKenzie, Tarra Anzalone, Chris Beaman, and Kyle Newman.
